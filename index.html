About ANTRIX

"Somewhere, something incredible is waiting to be known"
- Carl Sagan

The dangerous outer space is full of such incredibilities waiting to be discovered and marvelled at. We, at Antrix, aspire to achieve a lot beyond the daily hassles of life through the aspects of astronomy that shall otherwise seem unattached to daily life. We wish to ingrain a correlation between the seemingly untouched universe and our routines so as to develop ourselves. At Antrix, get ready to be beckoned by the lure of the unknown that the Universe provides in abundance!
